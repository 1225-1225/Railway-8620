`railway=*` is used to tag rails and infrastructure for many kinds of [railways](https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Railways) including light rail, mainline freight and industrial railways, [metros](https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Metros), monorails and [trams](https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Trams).
